Museum of Military Glory

On April 26, 2005, the opening of the entire museum complex in its entirety took place. At first in the courtyard of the museum building wanted to break up beautiful flower beds, but later plans changed. Instead of flowers, an interesting exposition of military equipment of different years appeared, but, basically, it is the equipment of the times The Great Patriotic War. It is located on the second floor of the Khoinik Museum of Local Lore. Inside the museum there is an exposition dedicated to the military glory of the Gomel region from ancient times to modern times. Here you can see weapons, uniforms, special equipment.

The museum also has a large collection of historical documents and rare photographs of the war years.The most colorful and elegant is the Victory Hall. The banners of the victorious military units of the Soviet Army are kept here.
