Kozel-Poklevsky Manor

The estate in the agro—town of Krasny Bereg is a small estate that is an example of architecture of the late XIX century. This estate is also called the Gatovsky or Kozel-Poklevsky estate. The architectural complex of the estate includes the main building of the owners of the estate, outbuildings and a park area. This estate is included in the list of objects of the tourist route "Golden Ring of Gomel region".

The history of this estate begins in the XVI century. This estate complex has changed many owners, but only under the Gatovskys and Kozel-Poklevskys the main building was erected in the eclectic style and a garden and park area was created.
