Gomel Regional Russian Drama Theater

The theater building opened in 1956 (a 1941 project). The rectangular volume has a symmetrical composition. Along the main axis there is a central entrance, a lobby group of rooms, an auditorium of the parterre-tiered type for 800 seats, a developed stage box. On the main facade there is a six-column portico with a pediment, numerous elements of classical architecture. The interiors of the main rooms are decorated with stucco. It was reconstructed in 2004-2005. The heating system of the building, porch and facades has been overhauled, the mixing console has been replaced.

In 1998, the museum was established. It contains information about the history of the theater, actors working at different times in the theater, tours, awards, anniversaries, as well as rare costumes. The museum is constantly updated with interesting materials.
