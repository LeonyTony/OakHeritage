Memorial complex "Krasny Bereg"

The Fascists occupied the Red Coast on July 5, 1941. In the village they created a large transit point. A military hospital was located here, in the former manor house of the pans, who needed large amounts of donated blood. In 1943, on the territory of the local educational farm , the Nazis organized a donor children's camp, through which about 15 thousand people passed, including 12 thousand children from different parts of Belarus and the USSR.

The complex is an Area of the Sun with eight radiating along the apple orchard alleys-rays. The main objects of the memorial are:

Sculpture of a girl

Emaciated and thin, the girl raises her thin arms in despair, as if trying to protect herself from all the nightmares and horrors of the Nazi "death factory".

"White class".

A composition of empty school desks in white. This class is also called dead.

A letter from Katya Susanina

The text of a real letter from a 15-year–old girl to her father at the front is on a blackboard in front of the "Dead Class". The letter was found in 1944 in Liozno as "the will of a dying daughter".The girl wrote it before committing suicide so as not to go to Germany.

The Ship of Hope

This symbol-monument is located in the center of the exposition. Children's names are inscribed on the snow-white sails.
