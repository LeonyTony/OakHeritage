Museum of Photographs

The Gomel photographer and collector Leonid Borisovich Pinsky was the first to present a large number of items to the museum. His initiative was supported not only by photographers and collectors from Gomel, but also from different parts of Belarus, Russia and other countries.

The permanent exhibition of the museum presents the key models photo equipment (Soviet Fotokor, Zenit, FED, Smena-8, Amateur, Kiev, famous German Leica, Spy Hit, Praktica, Argus, pavilion cameras, etc.), photographic materials, equipment, a large number of thematic books, periodicals on photography, accessories, as well as photographs and photo albums, personal belongings of famous photographers
