"Bagration" Memorial Complex

The height of the memorial sign is 7 m. In the center of the composition is a two–meter bronze sculpture group depicting the leaders of Operation Bagration, as well as the participants of those fights:partisans, soldiers, sailors of the Dnieper flotilla. On the right and on the left — the advancing soldiers. Installed near the village of Rakovichi, Svetlogorsk district Gomel region.It is from here that the Soviet troops A large-scale liberation of Belarusians has begun. "Bagration" entered the textbooks as the most successful offensive operation of the XX century.

In June 1944, the Soviet troops, using wetlocks, sledges, rafts and gati, presented the enemy with a deadly surprise. Tanks, people, artillery unexpectedly for the Germans passed through impassable swamps and caught the Nazis by surprise, with two main blows taking the German armies of the "Center" group in a pincer. As a result of the offensive, not only the territory of Belarus was liberated, but a 900-kilometer gap was formed between the German army groups "South" and "North".

Monument

The main materials of the memorial sign are red granite and bronze.

Leaders of Operation Bagration

Zhukov, Rokossovsky, Batov, Panov

Sailors and partisans

Right and left — advancing Soviet soldiers

Arrows

The monument is crowned with two bronze arrows depicting the directions of the main blows of Operation Bagration with the names of cities, liberated during the offensive of the Soviet troops.
