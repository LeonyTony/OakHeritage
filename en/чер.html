The Chernobyl Tragedy Museum

In May 2007, the museum received its first visitors. The tasks of the museum are: patriotic education of young people, perpetuating the memory of the heroes of Chernobyl, drawing public attention to the problems associated with the Chernobyl disaster and its consequences. It is located on the second floor of the Khoinik Museum of Local Lore.

One of the central places is occupied by the layout of the fourth power unit. On the opposite side, the reactor is literally burning. Once here, you instantly forget that it's just a backlight…

In a place of honor in one of the halls there are photos of 28 men.These are the firefighters who were the first to arrive at the scene of the emergency, stayed there for three hours and received a high dose of radiation. Personal protective equipment, personal and team dosimeters, a huge number of photos from 35 years ago, certificates, medals, most of the exhibits were transferred to the museum by liquidators or their relatives.

Personal protective equipment, personal and team dosimeters.

Hour

Anxiety spreads like a cold through the body. The numbers are blazing nearby – 01.23 is the very time when the explosion occurred. One by one, the stands tell how the usual course of events has changed for hundreds of thousands of families, millions of people.

Memory

A huge number of photos from 35 years ago

Hut

After an atmosphere of emergency liquidation of the consequences of the accident, museum visitors suddenly find themselves in an ordinary village hut. A woman is standing motionless by the stove, and there is absolute silence in the room. Everything froze. Just as life froze in the most affected settlements.
